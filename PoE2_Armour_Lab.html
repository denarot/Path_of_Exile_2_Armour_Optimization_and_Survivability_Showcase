🏹 Witchhunter — Sorcery Ward Calculator

Mechanics. Obsessive Rituals: 50% less armour and evasion; grants Sorcery Ward (SW) = 30% of post-penalty (armour + evasion), capped at 32,000. Ceremonial Ablution: SW covers all hit types — physical, elemental, and chaos. Sequence: armour DR first → SW absorbs post-DR remainder → ES → life takes overflow. SW recharges on a 6-second cooldown. Weapon-swap passive allocation lets culling strike and explode nodes sit on the secondary set at zero cost to the primary offensive allocation.
